NEED HELP?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and delivery options.

How do I join the Wine Club?
You can join our Wine Club by signing up on our website. Choose your subscription plan, and we’ll deliver handpicked bottles to your door each month.
What’s the difference between a vintage and a non-vintage wine?
Vintage wines are made from grapes harvested in a single year, reflecting that year’s unique conditions, while non-vintage wines blend grapes from multiple years for consistency.
Do I need a subscription to purchase wines?
No, you can purchase wines individually without a subscription. However, our Wine Club offers exclusive benefits and discounts.
Can I change my delivery preferences if needed?
Yes, you can modify your delivery preferences at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team.
Is my order information kept secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to ensure your order details and personal information are secure.
How long does shipping usually take?
Shipping typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location and the shipping option you choose.
What is your return policy?
If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact our support within 7 days for a full refund or replacement.
Can I gift a Wine Club subscription to someone else?
Yes! You can purchase gift subscriptions and have them delivered directly to the recipient’s address.
Do you offer tastings or events?
We host exclusive tastings and events for members. Keep an eye on our newsletter and event page for upcoming dates.
Are your wines organic or sustainably sourced?
We prioritize sourcing wines from organic and sustainable vineyards whenever possible to ensure quality and environmental care.
